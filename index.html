Simplified Usage

Even with just one slide, the component should handle the UI gracefully, showing only one active dot.
How to Develop & Goals Checklist

Ensure your play-list-project.js handles:

  • Focus Management: Clicking arrows or hitting "Enter" advances slides.
  • Responsive: Works on mobile and laptop screens.
  • DDD Design: Uses --ddd-spacing and --ddd-theme variables.
  • Wrap-around: Left arrow on slide 1 goes to last slide.
